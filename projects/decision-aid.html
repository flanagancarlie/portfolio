This is my favorite piece because it is the project where I both learned the most and had the largest impact. The website was created in collaboration with Worcester Polytechnic Institute and University of Massachusetts Chan Medical School. The tool was intended to be a guide for families of those suffering from traumatic brain injuries or similar illnesses, who need assistance in choosing treatment plans. Being a medical document, it was important to follow all accessibility standards and ensure the site was phone/tablet compatible, as well as being gentle in terminology to account for emotional users. I created the vector image on the home page and after numerous sessions of UI mockups and A/B testing, implemented a mobile-friendly navigation bar (screenshots below). Additionally, my work on the website led to an eye-tracking study being conducted between both schools.
